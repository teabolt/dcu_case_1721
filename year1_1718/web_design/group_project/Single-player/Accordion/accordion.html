Accordion

Object of the Game

To get all the cards in a pile.

The Deck

Standard 52-deck of cards that have been shuffled.

What is Dealt

The cards are dealt one by one face up, from left to right as many as the table can fit.

How to play

  • Any card on the table can be put on top of the next to its left ot the 3rd card to its left, if and only if the card is of the same suit or the same rank.
  • When one card is moved the intire pile is moved with the top card.
  • hypothetically if you have two 9's and they are suit of heart and club. Two may be on the Queen of hearts and then all of these cards can be put on the 6 of clubs.
