Emperor

Object of the Game

To obtain all eight foundations built up from ace to king

The Deck

Two standard 52-decks of cards that have been shuffled.

What is Dealt

10 piles of four cards, dealt in rows, the first 3 rows are face down. The last row if facing up. The remaining cards are left in a pile called the Stock.

How to play

  • Cards are flipped one at a time from the stock pile and put on the foundations. Cards that cannot be used are put into a waste pile however The top card of the waste pile is avilable.
  • All the aces are put above the foundations in a row as soon as a player see's it.
  • Top cards can only be moved. When moved the card below is then avilable. Cards may be put only on a card next-lower in rank and opposite colour.

Note

Kings cannot be placed on aces as ace if the lowest card in this situation.
