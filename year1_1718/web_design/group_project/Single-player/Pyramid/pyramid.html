Pyramid

Object of the Game

Clear the pyramid

The Deck

Standard 52-deck of cards that have been shuffled.

What is Dealt

Twenty-one cards are placed down in the formation of a pyramid. Lowest tier containing 7 cards.

How to play

  1. The lowest cards are exposed hence they can be used.
  2. At any time, any pair of available cards that total to 13 can be discard.
  3. The rest of the cards are put into a deck.
  4. These cards can be turned to aid you to total 13.
  5. If the card you turn over cannot be used it is discarded
  6. When the stock has be exhausted and no more airs of 13 can be used, the game ends..

Note

  • King = 13
  • Queen = 12
  • Jack = 11
  • Ace = 1
