Guess Who

Objective of the Game

The player has 24 face cards on their board and the object of the game is to be the first to determine which cards one's opponent has selected. Players alternate asking various yes or no question to try accomplish the objective.

Rules

  • The younger player always gets the the first turn.
  • Players alternate turns asking ye or no questions until one player makes a guess.
  • On your turn, you either have a choice of guessing the other players mystery card or you can ask a question.
  • You only get one chance to guess the other players mystery card, if you guess wrong you lose the game.
  • Continue asking questions until you are certain of the other players mystery card and if you guess correctly, you win and the game is over.
  • This game is designed for 2 players.

How to Play

Guess Who is a classic two player game that requires a player to have some type of strategy but there is also an element of luck involved. The player has to attempt to guess their opponents mystery card from 24 possibilities. You have to choose your questions wisely as to not give your opponent any clues of your card through your questions. The first player to guess correctly wins.

Challenge Game

For an extra challenge, the players can take two mystery cards and the objective is to guess both of your opponents mystery cards.

Rules

The rules stay the same except for some slight changes such as:

  • You must say "both" or "either" when asking questions about the mystery cards.
  • Be careful when flipping down faces and keep track of the questions you've asked.

Championship Play

If you decide to play a series of games, slide the score keeper up one point for every game that a player wins. The first to win five games is the champion.
