Meet LeafPad

The smart notebook, that will replace your old and dusty notepad forever. Made from paper but still friendly for the environment.

Read More

Same look,

but smarter

LeafPad looks like a standard notebook, but offers much more. You can store your notes on a cloud. The pressure sensible cover tracks your pen strokes and saves your notes on your favourite cloud service. Our smart notebook is unique, because you can use any pen and still get the same result.

Reusablity

Endlessly reusable

LeafPad provides 100% reusability (and 99% recyclability). After finishing writing just wipe the ink with our special cloth and write again and again....
