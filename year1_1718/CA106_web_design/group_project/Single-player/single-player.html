History

The first indictation of playing cards was in China in the 10th century. However there was no games that were recorded during this period. Playing cards arose again in the 12th century in Italy and Spain however they wer expensive beacuse of the work that went into the making of the cards, they had no printers so they had painters. Solitaire is also known as Patiences in some countrys like Germany, France, England and Poland. This game arose in the 18th century in the Baltic region in Europe. One player games became popular as years went buy as people enjoyed playing them and got quite addicting.
