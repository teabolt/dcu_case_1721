Solitaire

Object of the Game

Clear the table

The Deck

Standard 52-deck of cards that have been shuffled.

What is Dealt

The game obtains 7 column of cards, starting off with one card face up place 6 cards face down to the right. Move on to the second column and have one card face up and 5 cards face down. You do this until the last column. You sould end up having a pattern like this:

How to play

  1. You have 4 foundations piles and each pile contain only one suit.
  2. Once again aces accounts for once, hence king is the highest value. the foundations should start with ace and end with king.
  3. Cards in the columns can be moved to other columns only in decending order and must alternate between red and black.
  4. If you have an empty column you can then place any card there
  5. If you get stuck, you can then go the pile of cards you have left over and flip over 3 cards at a time and use that card.
