Connect Four

Objective of the Game

The objective of the game is to try and get four of your own coloured discs in either a horizontal, vertical or a diagonal line. The first player can always win if they play the right moves.

Rules

  • Before starting, players decide randomly which of them will be the beginner.
  • Moves are made alternatively and there is one move per turn.
  • This continues until one player gets four of their discs in a row.
  • The winner of the previous game is the player that gets the first turn on the next game.
  • This games is designed for 2 players.

How to Play

Connect Four is not an overly difficult game. You have to play until you get either four discs in a row or the board fills up which results in a draw. However to get four discs in a row without letting your opponent see what you're doing does require some skill, lots of strategy and also a bit of luck as you hope your opponent doesn't recognise a pattern.
